Our Story

Donut King  is a family owned donut shop that was opened in late 2007. Donuts are a family tradition for the owner, therefore you will get only the very best every time you visit with us.

We start with the freshest ingredients and handcraft each and every donut.

Our wide variety of donuts is sure to satisfy every customer, but if you don't see what you want, we will gladly make it for you. Our donuts are not our only specialty, we have freshly made sandwiches and house brewed coffee. We offer a full menu 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
